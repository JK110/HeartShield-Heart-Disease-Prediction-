About Our Model and Heart Health

Common Heart-Related Problems

Cardiovascular disease describes a range of conditions that affect your heart. These include:

  • Coronary Artery Disease: Damaged or diseased major blood vessels.
  • High Blood Pressure (Hypertension): The force of blood against your artery walls is consistently too high.
  • Arrhythmia: An irregular heartbeat, either too fast, too slow, or erratic.
  • Heart Failure: The heart doesn't pump blood as well as it should.

How This Model Can Help

This tool uses an AI machine learning model. It was trained on a large, anonymized dataset containing thousands of patient records, each with 11 health metrics (like age, blood pressure, cholesterol) and a known outcome (presence or absence of heart disease).

By analyzing your data, the model identifies complex patterns that are associated with higher risk. It provides a probability score, giving you an indication of potential risk based on the data it learned from. This is not a diagnosis, but a powerful tool for early warning and discussion.

Preventions and Healthy Activities

You can significantly reduce your risk of heart disease by adopting a healthier lifestyle:

Eat a Healthy Diet

Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Reduce intake of saturated fats, trans fats, sodium, and added sugars.

Stay Active

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous exercise each week.

Manage Stress

Find healthy ways to cope with stress, such as meditation, yoga, or spending time on hobbies.

Don't Smoke

Smoking is one of the most significant risk factors. Quitting can dramatically lower your risk.
